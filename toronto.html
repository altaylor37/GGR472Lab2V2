UP Express

This can be seen in more deatail in the map, but this is the route of the UP Express. It is an express rail service from Toronto - Pearson terminal 1 to Union Station in downtown Toronto. (Click the airport name on the home page to zoom in)

More Info

The UP Express is a 25-minute ride stopping along the way at Weston and Bloor (Transfer to TTC Line 2). The 23.3km ride opened in 2015 and is expected to open a new station at Mount Dennis to connected with the future TTC line 5.
